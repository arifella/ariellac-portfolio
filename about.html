About me

Welcome! I'm Ari, a Product Designer with 7+ years of experience across B2B and B2C healthcare companies, most recently at Maven Clinic.

I'm driven by the real people on the other end of the experiences I design, whether that's someone managing a health condition or a team trying to get their work done a little easier. I care just as much about the tangible line from design decisions to business outcomes as I do about the craft itself.

When I'm not at a screen, I'm off exploring Ohio's best spots, improvising recipes in the kitchen, and creating a tool to help families manage logistics for aging loved ones.

Thanks for checking out my portfolio! I'd love to connect and hear what you're building.
